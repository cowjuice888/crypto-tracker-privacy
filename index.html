Privacy Policy for Crypto Price Tracker

Last updated: August 13, 2025

Information We Collect

Crypto Price Tracker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users. The app operates entirely on your device.

Data Usage

  • The app fetches cryptocurrency price data from the CoinGecko public API
  • No personal data is sent to external servers
  • No user accounts or login information is required
  • No analytics or tracking is performed
  • No cookies or similar tracking technologies are used

Third-Party Services

This app uses the CoinGecko API to retrieve cryptocurrency prices. Please refer to CoinGecko's privacy policy for information about their data practices.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. No information is transmitted to our servers or any third parties beyond the necessary API calls to retrieve cryptocurrency prices.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required to fetch real-time cryptocurrency prices from the CoinGecko API
  • Network State: Used to check if internet connection is available

Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Users will be notified of any changes through app updates. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data, there is no personal information at risk. All data displayed in the app is public cryptocurrency pricing information.
